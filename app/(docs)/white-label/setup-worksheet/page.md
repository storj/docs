## Next Steps

Once you've completed this worksheet:

1. **Share your configuration** with your Storj account representative
2. **Set up DNS records** following the [DNS Configuration](/white-label/dns-configuration) guide
3. Storj will provision your white-label deployment and confirm when it's ready

For questions about specific fields, see [Defaults & Limits](/white-label/defaults) for more details on project limits and region selection.
